Account and Data Deletion

Tradelance allows users to delete their accounts and all associated data directly from within the app. When a user initiates the account deletion process:

  • All personal data, including name, email, contact details, and usage history, is permanently deleted from our servers.
  • The deletion is processed automatically and instantly with no data retained or backed up.
  • This process is irreversible, and all data will be removed from our databases.

To delete your account and data, open the Tradelance app and navigate to Profile > Settings > Delete Account and follow the prompts. If you need assistance, you can contact us at support@tradelance.com.
